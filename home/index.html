About the gym

Bouldering is an exciting sport where you put on specially made shoes with a sharp edge at the edge of your toes made from rubber. Then with the shous on you are tasked to climb up one of the many walls that are up to 5m high using a specially crafted rout. The routs are marked by different colors. Do note fear falling down even though bouldering means no ropers or harness to slow your fall since there is at least 60 cm thick, soft mat to catch your fall. Come and climb with us all gear is included in the pricing, but you can bring your own to.
